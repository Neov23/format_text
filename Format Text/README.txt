HOW TO USE:


#####


Steps:

1) Transfer "Format Text" folder in desired location (optimally in "C:\Program Files",
   but it doesn't actually matter)

2) Open "Format Text" that is stored in new, desired location

3) Right click on "Format Text.exe" file.

   NOTE!
   ".exe" extension might not be visible in your PC. It depends on your PC settings.

4) Click on "Create shortcut"

5) You can move the shortcut wherever you want. This is the file you will open,
   whenever you want to use the program.
